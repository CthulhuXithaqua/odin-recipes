Spaghetti all'assassina

Ingredients

  • spaghetti 320gr
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Extra-virgin olive oil, as needed
  • tomato sauce 300g
  • Water (hot), as needed
  • 1 chili pepper (or powder), as needed
  • salt, as needed

Steps

  1. Start by saut√©ing the garlic and chili pepper in a drizzle of oil (I used chili powder and added it later with the sauce)
  2. Add the spaghetti and immediately pour in hot water
  3. Add the hot tomato passata, remove the garlic, and then pour in a ladleful of sauce
  4. Adjust the salt to taste, then cook the spaghetti, adding sauce and water as needed, being careful not to let the bottom burn - this is their particularity
